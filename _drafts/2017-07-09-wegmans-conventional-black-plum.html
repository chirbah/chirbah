Reccomend? No, for the price, you're better off buying a peach.



I was encouraged by the fact that it looked like a butt.

This morning I tasted a Wegman's conventional black plum. For those of you not in the "know", the term conventional means "not organic". It seems a bit loaded, term-wise, since being conventional is not really a sought after designation in this age of being "different" and "cool", but we'll leave these distinctions up to the fruit linguists.



It was juicy, for sure. But not too sweet, and, unfortunately, a bit on the tart side. I can't really recommend this plum. Then again, it's my first plum of the summer, and I haven't yet tasted one that I liked.

I ate it anyway in cereal. Don't you want to see pictures of my cereal? Look, another stupid food blog!!
